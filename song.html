Sacred Wisdom

Arunachala Siva: Arunachala Siva represents the eternal truth, inner silence, and divine light. Devotees circumambulate Arunachala to dissolve karma and purify the soul.

Tirumala: Tirumala is home to Lord Venkateswara. Pilgrims visit for blessings, prosperity, and spiritual growth. The temple rituals inspire devotion and discipline.
