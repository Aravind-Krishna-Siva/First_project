Arunachala Siva

Arunachala Siva represents the eternal truth, inner silence, and the divine light that guides humanity toward self-realization. Arunachala is not merely a mountain but a living embodiment of Lord Siva Himself.

Devotees believe that circumambulating Arunachala dissolves karma, purifies the soul, and leads one closer to liberation. Arunachala Siva teaches that true greatness lies in stillness, surrender, and unwavering devotion.

His presence inspires humility, compassion, and the realization that the divine resides within all beings.

May Arunachala Siva bless all seekers.
