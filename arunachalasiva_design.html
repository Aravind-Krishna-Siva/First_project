Arunachala Siva

To all who behold these words: Arunachala Siva stands as the eternal beacon of light and inner silence—an embodiment of divine radiance and truth.

Devotees believe that circumambulating Arunachala dissolves karma, purifies the soul, and leads one closer to liberation. Arunachala Siva teaches that true greatness lies in stillness, surrender, and unwavering devotion.

✦ With devotion,
Eternal Seeker ✦
